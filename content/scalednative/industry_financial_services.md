AI for Financial Services Organizations

- Payment Processors

## Financial Services Compliance Checklist

Every AI implementation includes evaluation against:

- [ ] SR 11-7 / OCC 2011-12 model risk requirements
- [ ] Fair lending compliance (ECOA, Fair Housing)
- [ ] CFPB consumer protection requirements
- [ ] BSA/AML requirements
- [ ] State regulatory requirements
- [ ] SEC/FINRA requirements (if applicable)
- [ ] GLBA and privacy requirements
- [ ] Third-party risk management
