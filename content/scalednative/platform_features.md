## Feature 1: AI-Powered Personalization

### How It Works

When a learner begins training, Gemini AI creates a personalized learning experience based on:

| Factor | How It's Used |
|--------|---------------|
| **Role** | A developer sees code examples; a PM sees business cases |
| **Industry** | Healthcare workers see HIPAA scenarios; finance sees SOX |
| **Skill Level** | Beginners get more context; experts get accelerated paths |
| **Performance** | Struggling concepts get reinforced; mastered ones accelerate |
| **Learning Style** | Adjust content density, pacing, and examples |

### The Experience

**Before ScaledNative**: Everyone gets the same 60-minute video regardless of background.

**With ScaledNative**: The AI assesses your starting point and creates a custom path. A senior developer might skip fundamentals and dive into advanced patterns. A business analyst gets more conceptual framing before technical details.

### Adaptive in Real-Time

The AI watches engagement signals:
- Time spent on concepts
- Quiz performance
- Lab completion rates
- Question patterns

And adapts the content flow in real-time. No more suffering through content you already know. No more missing prerequisites you need.

## Feature 2: Organizational Context Integration

### The Problem with Generic Training

Generic AI training teaches generic examples:
- "Write a prompt to summarize this article"
- "Create a chatbot for customer service"

But your organization doesn't have generic needs. You have specific tools, policies, workflows, and domain knowledge.

### The ScaledNative Solution

Upload your organizational context:

| Context Type | What You Provide | How It's Used |
|--------------|------------------|---------------|
| **Policies** | AI usage policies, compliance docs | Compliance scenarios reference your actual rules |
| **Tools** | Your tech stack (Jira, Salesforce, etc.) | Examples use your actual tools |
| **Workflows** | Your business processes | Learners see AI applied to their real work |
| **Domain Docs** | Industry-specific knowledge | Domain-relevant examples and case studies |

### The Result

A developer learning prompt engineering sees examples using your actual codebase patterns. A sales rep learning AI for prospecting sees examples using your CRM and ICP. A compliance officer sees scenarios based on your regulatory environment.

Training becomes immediately applicable because it's training on your context.

## Feature 3: Adaptive Assessment Engine

### Beyond Multiple Choice

Traditional assessments:
- 10 multiple choice questions
- Same questions for everyone
- Pass/fail based on memorization
- No insight into actual capability

### ScaledNative Adaptive Assessments

Our AI-powered assessment engine:

**Adapts Difficulty in Real-Time**
- Answer correctly? Next question is harder.
- Struggle? The AI probes to find the gap.
- Result: Precise measurement of competency level.

**Identifies Weak Areas Automatically**
- The AI doesn't just say "you got 70%"
- It says "You understand prompting structure but struggle with context management"
- Then it recommends targeted remediation.

**Uses Varied Question Types**
- Scenario-based questions
- Code completion challenges
- Design critiques
- Real-world case analysis
- Not just recall, but application.

**Tracks Over Time**
- Spaced assessments detect skill decay
- The AI knows when you need a refresher
- Continuous verification, not one-time testing.

## Feature 4: Hands-On Skill Verification

### The Skills Gap Problem

Most training measures:
- Did they watch the video? ✓
- Did they pass the quiz? ✓

But that doesn't answer: Can they do the work?

### Interactive Labs with Automated Verification

ScaledNative includes hands-on labs for every critical skill:

**Code Sandboxes**
- Write real code in browser-based environments
- Automated verification checks correctness
- AI provides hints without giving answers

**AI Simulation Environments**
- Practice with actual AI models
- Experiment with prompts in controlled settings
- See real AI responses, not canned examples

**Scenario Simulations**
- Make decisions in realistic business scenarios
- AI evaluates reasoning, not just outcomes
- Branching paths based on choices

**Portfolio Building**
- Every lab generates proof of work
- Learners build a portfolio of demonstrated skills
- Managers can review actual output, not just scores

### Verification, Not Completion

When someone passes a ScaledNative lab, it means they demonstrated the skill. Not that they sat through content.

- Builds problem-solving skills, not dependency

**Knows Your Context**
- The tutor knows what module you're in
- It knows your performance history
- It tailors explanations to your level

**Available 24/7**
- No waiting for office hours
- No scheduling instructor time
- Instant, personalized guidance whenever needed

**Escalates When Needed**

- Instructors see the conversation and can jump in
- Seamless handoff between AI and human support
