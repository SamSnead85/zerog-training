# Role Module: AI for Marketing

## Module Title
AI for Marketing Professionals

## Purpose Statement
Transform marketing performance with AI. Learn to apply AI to content creation, personalization, analytics, and campaign optimization to drive growth and engagement.

## Who Is This For?

**Primary Audience**:
- Content Marketers
- Digital Marketers
- Demand Generation Managers
- Product Marketers
- Marketing Operations

**Secondary Audience**:
- CMOs and Marketing Directors
- Brand Managers
- Social Media Managers
- Marketing Analysts

## Key Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:

1. **Scale content production**: Use AI to create high-quality content at scale
2. **Enhance personalization**: Apply AI to segment-specific messaging and experiences
3. **Improve analytics**: Leverage AI for insight generation and attribution
4. **Optimize campaigns**: Use AI for testing, optimization, and performance
5. **Accelerate research**: Apply AI to competitive and market research

## Hands-On Project

### Capstone: Marketing AI Campaign

Design and execute an AI-enhanced marketing campaign:

1. **Campaign Strategy**: Define objectives and audience
2. **Content Creation**: Generate content with AI
3. **Personalization**: Create segment-specific variants
4. **Optimization**: Plan testing and optimization
5. **Analytics**: Define measurement and reporting
6. **Documentation**: Create campaign playbook

**Deliverable**: Complete campaign plan + content samples
