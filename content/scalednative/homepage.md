## How It Works Section

### Headline
See AI-Powered Learning in Action

### The ScaledNative Learner Journey

**Step 1: Personalized Onboarding**
When a learner joins, Gemini AI assesses their role, experience, and goals. The platform creates a personalized learning path, not a one-size-fits-all curriculum.

**Step 2: Adaptive Content Delivery**
Lessons adapt in real-time. If a learner struggles with a concept, the AI provides additional explanation, alternative examples, or prerequisite content. If they're advanced, the AI accelerates.

**Step 3: Context-Aware Examples**
Every example, case study, and scenario is infused with the learner's organizational context. A prompt engineering lesson uses examples from their industry and references their actual tools.

**Step 4: Interactive Skill Verification**
At each checkpoint, learners demonstrate competency in hands-on labs. Automated verification confirms they can do the work, not just recall information.

**Step 5: 24/7 AI Tutoring**
Stuck on a concept? The Socratic AI tutor provides guidance without giving away answers. It asks probing questions that lead learners to understanding.

**Step 6: Continuous Reinforcement**
Spaced repetition ensures skills stick. The platform automatically resurfaces concepts based on forgetting curves and performance data.
