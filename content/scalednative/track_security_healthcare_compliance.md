# Learning Track: AI Security, Healthcare, and Compliance

## Track Title
AI Security, Healthcare, and Compliance

## Purpose Statement
Ensure AI systems are secure, compliant, and safe for enterprise deployment. Deep coverage of AI security, healthcare-specific considerations (HIPAA), and regulatory compliance across industries.

## Who Is This For?

**Primary Audience**:
- Security Engineers
- Compliance Officers
- Privacy Officers
- Healthcare IT Professionals
- Risk Managers
- Legal/Regulatory Teams

**Secondary Audience**:
- CISO and Security Leaders
- Healthcare Administrators
- Audit Professionals
- Quality Assurance Teams

## Key Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:

1. **Secure AI systems**: Implement comprehensive security controls for LLM-based applications
2. **Ensure HIPAA compliance**: Deploy AI in healthcare settings with proper PHI protections
3. **Build governance frameworks**: Create AI governance that enables speed while managing risk
4. **Conduct AI audits**: Evaluate AI systems for security, bias, and compliance
5. **Manage AI risk**: Implement risk frameworks specific to AI technologies

## Prerequisites

- Navigate (Foundation) track completion
- 2+ years of experience in security, compliance, or healthcare IT
- Understanding of enterprise risk management concepts

This track covers:

## Curriculum Outline

### Module 1: AI Security Fundamentals (6 hours)
- The AI threat landscape
- OWASP Top 10 for LLMs
- Attack vectors unique to AI
- Defense in depth for AI systems
- Security architecture patterns
- **Lab**: Conduct threat modeling for an AI application

### Module 2: Prompt Security (8 hours)
- Prompt injection attacks (direct and indirect)
- Jailbreaking and model manipulation
- Defense strategies
- Input validation and sanitization
- Output filtering
- **Lab**: Test and defend against prompt injection

### Module 3: Data Security for AI (6 hours)
- Training data security
- User data in LLM contexts
- PII detection and handling
- Data encryption and access control
- Data retention and deletion
- **Lab**: Implement PII detection and masking

### Module 4: Healthcare AI Compliance (HIPAA) (10 hours)
- HIPAA fundamentals for AI
- PHI in AI contexts
- BAA requirements for AI vendors
- De-identification techniques
- Audit requirements
- Healthcare use case evaluation
- **Lab**: Design a HIPAA-compliant AI workflow

### Module 5: AI Governance Frameworks (8 hours)
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework
- ISO 42001 (AI Management System)
- Enterprise AI governance models
- Decision rights and accountability
- Policy development
- **Lab**: Create an AI governance framework

### Module 6: Bias and Fairness (6 hours)
- Types of AI bias
- Bias detection methods
- Fairness metrics
- Mitigation strategies
- Documentation and transparency
- **Lab**: Evaluate a model for bias

### Module 7: AI Audit and Assessment (8 hours)
- AI audit methodology
- Control testing for AI systems
- Continuous monitoring
- Third-party AI risk assessment
- Vendor due diligence
- **Lab**: Conduct an AI system audit

### Module 8: Regulatory Landscape (6 hours)
- EU AI Act
- US state AI regulations
- Industry-specific requirements (healthcare, finance, etc.)
- Compliance roadmap planning
- Regulatory monitoring
- **Lab**: Create a regulatory compliance checklist

### Module 9: Incident Response for AI (4 hours)
- AI-specific incident types
- Response procedures
- Communication protocols
- Post-incident review
- Continuous improvement
- **Lab**: Conduct an AI incident response drill

## Hands-On Project

### Capstone: AI Compliance Program

Develop a comprehensive AI compliance program for an organization:

1. **Risk Assessment**: Conduct enterprise AI risk assessment
2. **Governance Framework**: Design AI governance structure
3. **Security Controls**: Define security requirements for AI
4. **Compliance Mapping**: Map requirements to specific regulations
5. **Audit Program**: Design ongoing audit and monitoring
6. **Documentation**: Produce policy and procedure documentation

**Healthcare Variant**: Focus on HIPAA compliance for a healthcare AI use case

**Deliverable**: Complete AI compliance program documentation

**Verification**: Expert review against compliance standards
