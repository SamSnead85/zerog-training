# Industry Pack: AI for Government

## Industry Title
AI for Government Organizations

## Purpose Statement
Deploy AI across government organizations with proper attention to public trust, FedRAMP and security requirements, accessibility, and responsible AI mandates.

## Who Is This For?

**Organizations**:
- Federal Agencies
- State and Local Government
- Defense and Intelligence
- Public Utilities
- Government Contractors

**Roles**:
- Technology Leaders (CIO, CTO, CDO)
- Program Managers
- Contracting Officers
- Security/ISSO Personnel
- Policy Analysts
- Citizen Services Leaders

## Government-Specific Content Modules

### Module 1: Government AI Landscape (4 hours)
- AI applications across government (citizen services, operations, defense)
- Federal AI mandates and guidance (EO 14110, OMB memos)
- State and local AI initiatives
- Acquisition and procurement for AI
- Public trust considerations

### Module 2: FedRAMP and Security (8 hours)
- FedRAMP authorization for AI services
- Security requirements for AI (FISMA, NIST)
- Authority to Operate (ATO) considerations
- Continuous monitoring for AI
- Cloud security for AI workloads
- **Lab**: Security assessment for government AI

### Module 3: Responsible AI for Government (6 hours)
- Federal responsible AI requirements
- Bias and fairness in public services
- Transparency and explainability requirements
- Human oversight mandates
- AI impact assessments
- **Lab**: Conduct a government AI impact assessment

### Module 4: Citizen Services with AI (5 hours)
- Chatbots for citizen inquiries
- Form processing and automation
- Accessibility requirements (Section 508)
- Multi-language support
- Service delivery optimization
- **Lab**: Design an accessible citizen AI application

### Module 5: Government Operations (5 hours)
- Administrative AI applications
- Grants management
- FOIA processing
- Policy analysis
- Workforce applications
- **Lab**: Build an AI-assisted operations workflow

### Module 6: Acquisition and Procurement (4 hours)
- AI in government contracting
- RFP and proposal AI assistance
- Contract management
- Vendor evaluation for AI
- Market research with AI
- **Lab**: AI-assisted acquisition workflow

### Module 7: Government AI Governance (4 hours)
- Chief AI Officer role and responsibilities
- Agency AI governance frameworks
- Reporting requirements
- Algorithm inventory and management
- Cross-agency coordination
- **Lab**: Design a government AI governance program

## Government Compliance Checklist

Every AI implementation includes evaluation against:

- [ ] EO 14110 requirements
- [ ] OMB AI guidance
- [ ] FedRAMP authorization (for cloud AI)
- [ ] FISMA and NIST 800-53 controls
- [ ] Section 508 accessibility
- [ ] Privacy Act and E-Government Act
- [ ] Agency-specific AI policies
- [ ] Congressional reporting requirements
