# Learning Track: AI for Data and Analytics

## Track Title
AI for Data and Analytics

## Purpose Statement
Transform data and analytics workflows with AI. Learn to apply AI to data engineering, analytics, visualization, and decision support across the enterprise.

## Who Is This For?

**Primary Audience**:
- Data Analysts
- Business Intelligence Analysts
- Data Engineers
- Analytics Engineers
- Business Analysts

**Secondary Audience**:
- Data Scientists
- Product Analysts
- Finance Analysts
- Operations Analysts

## Key Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:

1. **Augment data workflows**: Apply AI to data cleaning, transformation, and pipeline development
2. **Accelerate analysis**: Use AI for exploratory analysis, insight discovery, and hypothesis generation
3. **Enhance visualization**: Create AI-assisted dashboards and narratives
4. **Democratize data**: Build natural language interfaces for data querying
5. **Improve data quality**: Apply AI to data quality monitoring and remediation

## Prerequisites

- Navigate (Foundation) track completion
- 1+ years of experience in data/analytics
- Proficiency with SQL
- Familiarity with at least one BI tool
