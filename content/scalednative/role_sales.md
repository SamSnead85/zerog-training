# Role Module: AI for Sales

## Module Title
AI for Sales Professionals

## Purpose Statement
Transform sales performance with AI. Learn to apply AI to prospecting, deal intelligence, sales enablement, and forecasting to close more deals and exceed quota.

## Who Is This For?

**Primary Audience**:
- Account Executives
- Business Development Representatives
- Sales Managers
- Sales Engineers
- Solution Consultants

**Secondary Audience**:
- VP Sales and CROs
- Sales Operations
- Revenue Operations
- Partner Sales

## Key Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:

1. **Improve prospecting**: Use AI for lead research, personalization, and outreach
2. **Enhance deal intelligence**: Apply AI to understand buyer needs and competitive dynamics
3. **Accelerate preparation**: Leverage AI for meeting prep, proposal creation, and presentation
4. **Improve forecasting**: Use AI for pipeline analysis and deal scoring
5. **Scale enablement**: Apply AI to coaching, training, and knowledge management

## Hands-On Project

Design and implement an AI workflow for your sales process:

1. **Process Mapping**: Identify high-impact AI opportunities
2. **Tool Selection**: Choose tools that integrate with your stack
3. **Workflow Design**: Design AI-assisted workflows
4. **Measurement**: Define KPIs and tracking
5. **Implementation**: Create rollout plan
6. **Scale**: Plan for team adoption

**Deliverable**: Working sales AI workflow + documentation
