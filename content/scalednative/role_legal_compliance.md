# Role Module: AI for Legal and Compliance

## Module Title
AI for Legal and Compliance Professionals

## Purpose Statement
Transform legal and compliance operations with AI. Learn to apply AI to contract management, legal research, compliance monitoring, and regulatory affairs while maintaining professional standards and ethical obligations.

## Who Is This For?

**Primary Audience**:
- In-House Counsel
- Corporate Lawyers
- Paralegals
- Compliance Officers
- Contract Managers

**Secondary Audience**:
- General Counsels
- Chief Compliance Officers
- Regulatory Affairs
- Risk Managers

## Key Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:

1. **Accelerate contract work**: Use AI for contract review, drafting, and management
2. **Enhance legal research**: Apply AI to case law, regulatory, and precedent research
3. **Improve compliance monitoring**: Leverage AI for policy monitoring and risk detection
4. **Streamline legal operations**: Use AI for matter management and e-discovery
5. **Maintain professional standards**: Apply AI within ethical and privilege requirements

## Hands-On Project

### Capstone: Legal AI Implementation

Design and document an AI implementation for a specific legal use case:

1. **Use Case Selection**: Choose contracts, research, compliance, or e-discovery
2. **Requirements**: Define accuracy and risk tolerance
3. **Solution Design**: Design AI-assisted workflow
4. **Ethics Review**: Address privilege and ethical requirements
5. **Implementation Plan**: Create rollout plan
6. **Measurement**: Define success metrics

**Deliverable**: Complete implementation plan + ethics documentation
