## Assessment Dimensions

### The Assessment Evaluates Four Dimensions:

**Dimension 1: Individual AI Adoption**
- What percentage of employees are actively using AI in their work?
- Is usage compliant and governed?
- Do individuals have formal AI training?
- Are skills verified or self-reported?

**Dimension 2: Team AI Workflows**
- Do teams have documented AI-enhanced workflows?
- Are there team-level AI standards?
- Do teams measure AI productivity impact?
- Are there team champions driving adoption?

**Dimension 3: Organizational Standards**
- Does your organization have formal AI governance?
- Are there cross-department AI standards?
- Is AI usage tracked and measured organization-wide?
- Are there clear policies for AI tools and data?

**Dimension 4: Executive Ownership**
- Is there executive sponsorship for AI transformation?
- Is AI part of strategic planning?
- Are investments coordinated across the enterprise?
- Is there a roadmap to AI-Native operations?

## What Happens After the Assessment

### Your Path Forward

**If You're at Level 1 (Foundations)**
Focus on individual AI literacy. Build the base of certified practitioners. Establish initial governance.

**If You're at Level 2 (Teams)**
Move from individual adoption to team workflows. Identify and enable team champions. Standardize team practices.

**If You're at Level 3 (Organization)**
Build cross-functional alignment. Establish enterprise standards. Formalize governance and metrics.

**If You're at Level 4 (Operating Model)**
Optimize and sustain. Focus on continuous improvement. Lead your industry.
