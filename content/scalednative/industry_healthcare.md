# Industry Pack: AI for Healthcare

## Industry Title
AI for Healthcare Organizations

## Purpose Statement
Deploy AI across healthcare organizations with proper attention to patient safety, HIPAA compliance, clinical workflow integration, and regulatory requirements.

## Who Is This For?

**Organizations**:
- Health Systems and Hospitals
- Physician Groups
- Health Plans and Payers
- Pharma and Life Sciences
- Digital Health Companies

**Roles**:
- Healthcare IT Leaders
- Clinical Informaticists
- Compliance Officers
- Medical Directors
- Healthcare Administrators

## Healthcare-Specific Content Modules

### Module 1: Healthcare AI Landscape (4 hours)
- AI applications in healthcare (clinical, operational, administrative)
- Regulatory landscape (FDA, HHS, state regulations)
- EHR and clinical system integration
- Patient safety considerations
- Healthcare AI adoption trends

### Module 2: HIPAA and Healthcare Compliance (8 hours)
- HIPAA fundamentals for AI
- Protected Health Information (PHI) in AI contexts
- Business Associate Agreements (BAA) for AI vendors
- De-identification techniques and standards
- Audit trail requirements
- State privacy law variations
- **Lab**: HIPAA compliance assessment for AI use case

### Module 3: Clinical Workflow Integration (6 hours)
- EHR integration patterns (Epic, Cerner, etc.)
- Clinical decision support with AI
- Documentation and coding assistance
- Care coordination applications
- Patient communication
- **Lab**: Design an AI-enhanced clinical workflow

### Module 4: Administrative and Revenue Cycle (5 hours)
- Prior authorization automation
- Coding and billing assistance
- Denial management
- Patient access and scheduling
- Revenue cycle analytics
- **Lab**: Build an AI-assisted prior auth workflow

### Module 5: Population Health and Analytics (5 hours)
- Risk stratification with AI
- Care gap identification
- Social determinants of health
- Predictive analytics for population health
- Quality measure reporting
- **Lab**: Create a population health AI model

### Module 6: Healthcare AI Governance (4 hours)
- Clinical AI governance frameworks
- Model validation for clinical use
- Bias monitoring in healthcare AI
- Physician and patient trust
- Transparency and explainability
- **Lab**: Design a healthcare AI governance program

## Healthcare Compliance Checklist

Every AI implementation includes evaluation against:

- [ ] HIPAA Privacy Rule compliance
- [ ] HIPAA Security Rule compliance
- [ ] BAA requirements for AI vendors
- [ ] De-identification standards (Safe Harbor, Expert Determination)
- [ ] State health privacy laws
- [ ] FDA considerations (if clinical decision support)
- [ ] Patient consent requirements
- [ ] Audit logging and access controls
