# Executive Toolkit: AI Training Vendor Evaluation Framework

## Purpose
Provide a structured framework for CIOs and L&D leaders to evaluate and select AI training vendors.

---

## Executive Summary

When evaluating AI training vendors, focus on five dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | Key Questions |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| **Content Quality** | 30% | Is content deep, current, and role-based? |
| **Delivery Mechanism** | 25% | Is there personalization and hands-on verification? |
| **Enterprise Readiness** | 20% | Does it integrate with our systems and meet security requirements? |
| **Organizational Impact** | 15% | Does it drive transformation, not just training? |
| **Vendor Viability** | 10% | Is the vendor stable and continuously investing? |

## Reference Check Questions

### For Existing Customers

**Adoption and Impact**:
1. What percentage of your target population completed training?
2. How did you measure business impact?
3. What was the actual ROI vs. projected?

**Implementation**:
4. How long did implementation take?
5. What were the biggest challenges?
6. How responsive was vendor support?

**Content and Quality**:
7. How do learners rate the content quality?
8. Is the content staying current?
9. Are the hands-on labs effective?

**Recommendation**:
10. Would you select this vendor again?
11. What would you do differently?

---

## RFP Questions Template

### Content and Curriculum

1. Describe the total curriculum: hours, modules, and tracks.
2. How often is content updated? Provide changelog for last 12 months.
3. Who creates your content? Provide credentials.
4. Describe role-based learning paths available.
5. Provide sample module for evaluation.

### Delivery and Technology

6. Describe personalization capabilities.
7. How are skills verified? Provide lab examples.
8. Describe AI/ML technology used in delivery.
9. What is the learner support model?

### Enterprise Integration

10. Describe security certifications and compliance.
11. What SSO options are supported?
12. Describe HRIS integration capabilities.
13. Describe LMS integration options.
14. What analytics and reporting are available?

### Framework and Transformation

15. Describe your transformation framework.
16. How do you support organizational governance?
17. What change management resources are available?

### Commercial

18. Describe pricing model and tiers.
19. What is included in implementation?
20. What is the renewal structure?

## Red Flags to Watch For

**Content Red Flags**:
- Only basic content (< 20 hours)
- No role-based paths
- Content older than 6 months
- No hands-on components

**Delivery Red Flags**:
- No personalization
- Completion-only measurement
- No skill verification
- Poor learner experience

**Enterprise Red Flags**:
- No SOC 2 or equivalent
- Basic SSO only
- No API access
- Limited reporting

**Vendor Red Flags**:
- No enterprise references
- Limited product roadmap
- High employee turnover
- Financial instability indicators
