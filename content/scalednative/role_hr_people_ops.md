# Role Module: AI for HR and People Operations

## Module Title
AI for HR and People Operations

## Purpose Statement
Transform HR operations with AI. Learn to apply AI responsibly to recruiting, employee experience, workforce planning, and people analytics while maintaining ethical standards and legal compliance.

## Who Is This For?

**Primary Audience**:
- HR Business Partners
- Talent Acquisition Specialists
- People Operations Managers
- L&D Professionals
- HR Analysts

**Secondary Audience**:
- CHROs and HR Directors
- Compensation and Benefits Specialists
- Employee Experience Managers
- Workforce Planning Analysts

## Key Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:

1. **Accelerate recruiting**: Use AI for sourcing, screening, and candidate communication
2. **Enhance employee experience**: Apply AI to employee inquiries, onboarding, and support
3. **Improve workforce planning**: Leverage AI for skills analysis and capacity planning
4. **Elevate people analytics**: Use AI for insight generation and reporting
5. **Ensure ethical AI use**: Apply responsible AI principles to HR applications

## Curriculum Outline

### Module 1: AI Landscape for HR (3 hours)
- HR tech AI landscape
- Opportunities and risks
- Ethical considerations unique to HR
- Legal and compliance requirements (EEOC, bias)
- Building the case for AI in HR

### Module 2: AI in Recruiting (6 hours)
- Sourcing automation
- Resume screening (with bias mitigation)
- Interview scheduling and coordination
- Candidate communication
- Assessment and evaluation support
- **Lab**: Build an AI-assisted recruiting workflow

### Module 3: Employee Experience and Support (5 hours)
- HR chatbots and virtual assistants
- Policy Q&A automation
- Onboarding automation
- Self-service support
- Personalized employee communication
- **Lab**: Design an HR virtual assistant

### Module 4: Learning and Development (4 hours)
- AI-powered skill gap analysis
- Personalized learning recommendations
- Content curation and generation
- Training effectiveness measurement
- Career path recommendations
- **Lab**: Create a skills-based learning recommendation

### Module 5: Workforce Planning (4 hours)
- Skills taxonomy with AI
- Capacity and demand planning
- Succession planning support
- Organizational network analysis
- Future workforce modeling
- **Lab**: Build a skills analysis dashboard

### Module 6: People Analytics with AI (4 hours)
- AI-assisted reporting
- Insight generation from HR data
- Predictive analytics (attrition, performance)
- Natural language queries for HR data
- Executive storytelling
- **Lab**: Create an AI-enhanced people analytics report

### Module 7: Responsible AI in HR (4 hours)
- Bias in HR AI systems
- Fairness testing and monitoring
- Transparency and explainability
- Legal compliance
- Governance frameworks
- **Lab**: Conduct bias audit of HR AI tool

## Hands-On Project

### Capstone: HR AI Implementation

Design and document an AI implementation for a specific HR use case:

1. **Use Case Selection**: Choose recruiting, employee experience, or analytics
2. **Requirements**: Define success criteria and constraints
3. **Solution Design**: Design AI-assisted workflow
4. **Ethics Review**: Conduct responsible AI assessment
5. **Implementation Plan**: Create rollout plan
6. **Measurement**: Define success metrics

**Deliverable**: Complete implementation plan + responsible AI documentation

- Self-paced modules: 28 hours
- Hands-on labs: 3 hours
