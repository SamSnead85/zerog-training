# Role Module: AI for Customer Success

## Module Title
AI for Customer Success Professionals

## Purpose Statement
Transform customer success performance with AI. Learn to apply AI to health scoring, churn prediction, renewal management, and customer support to drive retention and expansion.

## Who Is This For?

**Primary Audience**:
- Customer Success Managers
- Account Managers
- Customer Support Representatives
- Implementation Specialists
- Technical Account Managers

**Secondary Audience**:
- VP Customer Success
- Support Managers
- Customer Operations
- Customer Experience

## Key Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:

1. **Predict and prevent churn**: Use AI for early warning detection and intervention
2. **Scale customer support**: Apply AI to ticket handling and knowledge management
3. **Improve health scoring**: Leverage AI for multi-signal health assessment
4. **Accelerate onboarding**: Use AI for customer enablement and documentation
5. **Drive expansion**: Apply AI to identify and pursue growth opportunities
