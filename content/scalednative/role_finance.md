# Role Module: AI for Finance

## Module Title
AI for Finance Professionals

## Purpose Statement
Transform finance operations with AI. Learn to apply AI to financial planning and analysis, accounting, audit, treasury, and investor relations while maintaining accuracy and compliance.

## Who Is This For?

**Primary Audience**:
- Financial Analysts
- FP&A Professionals
- Accountants
- Internal Auditors
- Treasury Analysts

**Secondary Audience**:
- CFOs and Finance Directors
- Controllers
- Investor Relations
- Risk Management

## Key Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:

1. **Accelerate FP&A**: Use AI for forecasting, variance analysis, and scenario modeling
2. **Enhance reporting**: Apply AI to financial reporting and analysis
3. **Improve audit efficiency**: Leverage AI for audit procedures and testing
4. **Streamline accounting**: Use AI for transaction processing and reconciliation
5. **Ensure compliance**: Apply AI within SOX, GAAP, and regulatory requirements

## Hands-On Project

### Capstone: Finance AI Implementation

Design and document an AI implementation for a specific finance use case:

1. **Use Case Selection**: Choose FP&A, reporting, accounting, or audit
2. **Requirements**: Define accuracy requirements and constraints
3. **Solution Design**: Design AI-assisted workflow
4. **Control Design**: Ensure SOX and compliance requirements
5. **Implementation Plan**: Create rollout plan
6. **Measurement**: Define success metrics

**Deliverable**: Complete implementation plan + compliance documentation
