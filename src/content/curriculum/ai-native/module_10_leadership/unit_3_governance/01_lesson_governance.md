### Governance Structure

**AI Governance Board**:
- Executive sponsor
- Legal/Compliance
- Security
- Ethics representative
- Business stakeholders
- Technical leads

### Policy Framework

**Tier 1: Unrestricted**
- Internal productivity tools
- Low-risk automation
- No customer data

**Tier 2: Standard Review**
- Customer-facing AI
- Recommendations
- Content generation

**Tier 3: Ethics Review**
- Decisions affecting people
- High-stakes outcomes
- Sensitive domains (healthcare, finance)
