### Core Ethical Principles

| Principle | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **Fairness** | No discrimination, equal treatment |
| **Transparency** | Explainable decisions |
| **Privacy** | Data protection, consent |
| **Accountability** | Clear responsibility |
| **Safety** | Prevent harm |
| **Human Agency** | Human control over AI |

### Bias and Fairness

**Sources of Bias**:
- Training data imbalances
- Historical discrimination in data
- Proxy variables for protected classes

**Mitigation**:
- Diverse training data
- Fairness testing across groups
- Regular bias audits
- Human oversight for critical decisions

### Transparency Requirements

**When to Explain**:
- AI vs human decisions
- How AI affects outcomes
- Right to appeal/override

### Responsible AI Checklist

- [ ] Bias testing completed
- [ ] Explainability documented
- [ ] Privacy review passed
- [ ] Human oversight defined
- [ ] Appeal process exists
- [ ] Monitoring in place
