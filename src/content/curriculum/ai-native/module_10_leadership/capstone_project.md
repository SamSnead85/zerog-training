# Module 10 Capstone Project: AI Transformation Plan

> **Duration**: 6-8 hours | **Type**: Comprehensive Assessment
> **Submission**: Strategic Plan + Presentation

---

## Project Overview

Develop a comprehensive AI transformation strategy for an organization.

---

## Requirements

### Part 1: Vision & Strategy (2 hours)

- AI vision statement
- 3-year roadmap
- Build vs buy analysis
- Competitive positioning

### Part 2: Organization Design (2 hours)

- Team structure recommendation
- Key roles and hiring plan
- Training program outline
- Culture initiatives

### Part 3: Governance (1 hour)

- Governance framework
- Risk-tiered approval process
- Ethical guidelines
- Accountability structure

### Part 4: Value & Adoption (2 hours)

- ROI model and projections
- Success metrics
- Change management plan
- Executive communication

### Part 5: Presentation (1 hour)

- Executive summary (5 slides)
- Detailed plan document
- Investment ask
