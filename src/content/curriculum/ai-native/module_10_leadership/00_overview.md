# Module 10: AI Leadership and Strategy

> **Duration**: 8 hours | **Certification Path**: Executive, AI Leader
> **Prerequisites**: Module 1 (minimum)

---

## Module Overview

Leading AI transformation requires understanding technology, strategy, and organizational change. This module covers the executive perspective on building AI-native organizations.

---

## Learning Objectives

1. Develop AI vision and strategy
2. Build and lead AI teams
3. Navigate AI governance and ethics
4. Measure and communicate AI value
5. Drive organizational AI adoption
