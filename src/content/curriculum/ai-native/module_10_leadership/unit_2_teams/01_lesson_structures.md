### Team Models

| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Centralized** | Single AI team serves all | Efficiency, consistency |
| **Embedded** | AI specialists in each team | Speed, domain expertise |
| **Hub-and-Spoke** | Central CoE + embedded | Balance |
| **Federated** | Guidelines, distributed execution | Large orgs |

### Key Roles

| Role | Focus |
|------|-------|
| AI/ML Engineer | Building models & systems |
| Prompt Engineer | LLM optimization |
| Data Engineer | Data pipelines |
| MLOps | Deployment, monitoring |
| AI Product Manager | Strategy, roadmap |
| AI Ethics Lead | Governance, responsibility |

### Team Composition (Starter)

**Small Team (3-5)**:
- 1 AI/ML Engineer
- 1 Data Engineer
- 1 Product Manager
- 1-2 Domain experts (part-time)

**Growth Team (10-15)**:
- 3-4 AI Engineers
- 2 Data Engineers
- 1-2 MLOps
- 2 Product Managers
- 1 Technical Lead
- Support from Legal, Security
