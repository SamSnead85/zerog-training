### AI-Friendly Culture

| Characteristic | Why It Matters |
|----------------|----------------|
| Experimentation | AI requires iteration |
| Failure tolerance | Not all experiments work |
| Data-driven | AI needs quality data |
| Cross-functional | AI touches everything |
| Learning-oriented | Field changes rapidly |

### Enabling Innovation

**Dedicated Time**:
- 20% time for exploration
- Hackathons and innovation days
- Learning budget per person

**Safe to Experiment**:
- Sandboxed environments
- Clear guardrails, not blockers
- Celebrate learnings, not just wins

### Change Management

**Building AI Awareness**:
1. Executive sponsorship
2. Success story sharing
3. Training at all levels
4. AI champions in each team

**Addressing Fear**:
- Be transparent about AI impact
- Focus on augmentation, not replacement
- Reskilling commitments
- Involve employees in AI projects
