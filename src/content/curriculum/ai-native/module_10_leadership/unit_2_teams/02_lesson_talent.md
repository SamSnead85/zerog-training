### Hiring Strategy

**Build vs Hire**:
- Upskill existing engineers (faster, cultural fit)
- Hire specialists for critical gaps
- Partner with consultants for quick starts

### What to Look For

| Role | Key Indicators |
|------|----------------|
| AI Engineer | Projects, not just credentials |
| Prompt Engineer | Creative problem-solving |
| Data Engineer | Scale, reliability experience |
| AI PM | Cross-functional experience |

### Growing Internal Talent

**AI Upskilling Program**:
1. Foundations (2 weeks): AI concepts, prompting
2. Hands-on (4 weeks): Build projects
3. Specialization (6 weeks): Deep dive
4. Production (ongoing): Real projects

### Retention Strategies

- Challenging, impactful projects
- Learning and growth opportunities
- Competitive compensation
- Visibility to leadership
- Community and collaboration
