### Defining AI Vision

An AI vision answers: **How will AI transform our business?**

**Good Vision Example**:
> "Within 3 years, we will leverage AI to deliver personalized experiences to every customer, automate 60% of routine operations, and enable our teams to focus on high-value creative work."

### AI Maturity Stages

| Stage | Description | Focus |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| **Experimenting** | Pilots and POCs | Learning |
| **Operationalizing** | Production use cases | Scaling |
| **Transforming** | AI-native processes | Innovation |
| **Leading** | Competitive differentiation | Industry impact |

### Building the Roadmap

**Phase 1: Foundation (0-6 months)**
- Data infrastructure assessment
- Skill gap analysis
- Governance framework
- 2-3 pilot projects

**Phase 2: Scale (6-18 months)**
- Production deployments
- Center of Excellence
- Enterprise AI platform
- Training programs

**Phase 3: Transform (18-36 months)**
- AI-native processes
- New AI-enabled products
- Industry leadership
- Continuous innovation

### Strategic Questions

1. What problems are most valuable to solve with AI?
2. Where does AI create competitive advantage?
3. What capabilities do we need to build vs buy?
4. How will AI change our workforce?
