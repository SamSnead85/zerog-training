### Calculating ROI

**Cost Side**:
- Development costs
- API/infrastructure costs
- Training costs
- Maintenance costs

**Value Side**:
- Hours saved Ã— hourly cost
- Revenue increase

**ROI Formula**:

### Measurement Framework

**Leading Indicators** (track early):
- Adoption rate
- Usage frequency
- User satisfaction

**Lagging Indicators** (business impact):
- Revenue impact
- Cost reduction
- Quality improvement

### Communicating Value

**Executive Dashboard**:
- Total AI investment
- Total value generated
- ROI by initiative
- Strategic impact summary
