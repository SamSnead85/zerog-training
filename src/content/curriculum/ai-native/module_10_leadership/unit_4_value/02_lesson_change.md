### AI Adoption Challenges

| Challenge | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Resistance to change | Early involvement, transparency |
| Lack of trust in AI | Explainability, human oversight |
| Skill gaps | Training programs |
| Process disruption | Phased rollout |

### Adoption Framework

**Phase 1: Awareness**
- Executive communication
- Town halls and demos
- Success story sharing

**Phase 2: Enablement**
- Training programs
- Tools and access
- Support resources

**Phase 3: Adoption**
- Pilot groups
- Feedback loops
- Iterate and improve

**Phase 4: Scale**
- Broad rollout
- Champions network
- Continuous improvement

### Stakeholder Management

**Identify**:
- Champions (promote actively)
- Supporters (need updates)
- Neutral (need convincing)
- Resistors (need listening)

**Engage Each**:
- Champions: Give them visibility
- Resistors: Address concerns directly

### Success Metrics

- % of employees using AI tools
- Satisfaction with AI
- Time to competency
- Support ticket trends
