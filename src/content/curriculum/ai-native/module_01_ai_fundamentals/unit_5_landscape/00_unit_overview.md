# Unit 5: The Landscape of AI

> **Unit Duration**: 3.5 hours | **Lessons**: 4 + Assessment
> **Module**: 1 - AI Fundamentals for Modern Development

---

## Unit Overview

The AI ecosystem is vast and evolving rapidly. This unit maps the major players, tools, and platforms you need to know—from foundation model providers to deployment infrastructure.

---

## Learning Objectives

By completing this unit, you will be able to:

1. Identify the major AI model providers and their offerings
2. Compare open-source vs proprietary models for different use cases
3. Navigate the ecosystem of AI development tools and frameworks
4. Evaluate deployment options: API, cloud, self-hosted
5. Anticipate where the AI landscape is heading

---

---

## Why This Matters

The AI landscape changes monthly. New models, frameworks, and providers emerge constantly. This unit gives you a framework for evaluating options and staying current—not just a snapshot that becomes outdated.
